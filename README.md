# 📦 Inventory & Supply Chain Management Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
The **Inventory & Supply Chain Management Analysis Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of inventory performance, warehouse efficiency, sales trends, transportation costs, lead times, and order fulfillment status across regions and product categories.  
It is designed to help businesses **optimize inventory, reduce costs, and improve supply chain decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- Monitor warehouse utilization and inventory health
- Analyze sales and inventory trends over time
- Identify transportation cost patterns across regions
- Track lead times and backorders
- Support data-driven supply chain optimization

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
| Metric | Value | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| Warehouse Utilization | **34.08%** | Percentage of warehouse capacity currently used |
| Days Sales of Inventory (DSI) | **15.56 days** | Average number of days inventory remains before sale |
| Inventory Turnover | **23.47** | Frequency of inventory replenishment |

## 📈 Visual Insights

### 1️⃣ Warehouse Utilization Gauge
- Displays current warehouse capacity usage
- Helps detect underutilization or overcapacity risks

### 2️⃣ Transportation Cost by Region & Category
- Regions: **North, West, East, South**
- Categories: **Accessories, Clothing, Electronics, Furniture**
- Identifies high-cost regions and product segments

### 3️⃣ Units Sold by Year
- Time range: **2020 – 2024**
- Highlights sales growth trends and demand recovery
- Significant growth observed after 2021

### 4️⃣ Average Lead Time by Category (Days)
| Category | Avg Lead Time |
|--------|--------------|
| Accessories | 16.60 |
| Electronics | 15.68 |
| Furniture | 15.50 |
| Clothing | 15.29 |

- Useful for supplier performance evaluation

### 5️⃣ Backorder Status Distribution
| Status | Count |
|------|------|
| Fulfilled | 838 |
| Pending | 248 |
| Canceled | 114 |

- Helps identify order fulfillment efficiency and bottlenecks

### 6️⃣ Inventory Level by Category & Region
- Compares stock levels across regions
- Enables demand balancing and inventory redistribution
